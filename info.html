Welcome to Last Prop Stadning!

Press 'Q' to view your current keybinds,
'F1' to view this menu and 'F2' to switch teams.


Useful Commands:

  • !stuck (Will attempt to get you unstuck.)
  • !options (Show player options)
  • !bindings (Edit you current keybinds)
  • !rtv (Will vote to initiate a mapvote)
